DesignCrowd Project Brief Redesign.

Task

To improve the design of the existing brief page. The current brief page is an old page on DesignCrowd and we want to give it an upgrade. We want to focus on improving the design so it fits with the look and feel on our homepage (http://www.designcrowd.com) in addition to improving how we communicate elements on the page.

Key Objectives

  • Improve the look and feel
  • Make it easier for designers to understand the status of the project (open, closed etc.), how many designs received and how many days left.
  • Increase the prominence on amount of money on offer
  • Enhance the prominence and look of the call to action to submit design
  • Deliver either a PhotoShop (PSD) or implemented HTML as a final deliverable

Tasks Completed

  • Analysed design and identified key areas for improvement
  • Completed lo-fi wireframes on paper to establish a direction for new design
  • Mocked up high detailed visual designs in Photoshop for guidance
  • Built out frontend using existing DesignCrowd assets where possible

Package Contents

  • PSD files for Project Brief page, Signed In and Signed Out states
  • Sprites PSD
  • All HTML, CSS and Images

Notes

Due to time constraints:

  • Testing and development was limited to just one browser - Chrome
  • Media Queries are not supported in this build. The build was tested at 1170px only.
  • No JavaScript functionality was implemented as the task was focused more on design & UX.
  • A design for a signed out user exists but there is no markup to accompany it.
  • Some fonts may not render correctly as the correct weight may not be installed.
